Welcome to the Book Talk club


Hello, book lovers and English language enthusiasts!


We're thrilled to have you join our community dedicated to improving English skills through the joy of reading. Whether you're a beginner looking to build vocabulary or an advanced learner aiming to refine your comprehension, you've come to the right place.


Ready to dive into our current book selection? Check out our "Genre groups" section to get started.


What do we do here


Here, we explore captivating stories, discuss thought-provoking themes, and support each other in our language learning journey. Our book club offers a friendly, encouraging environment where you can practice your English, share your thoughts, and connect with fellow readers from around the world.


How do I get into the club


Joining our English practice book club is easy and fun! Simply create a free account on our website to gain access to all our features. Once registered, browse our current book selections and choose the one that interests you most. We offer options for various English proficiency levels in different genres, so you'll find something suitable regardless of your current skills. After selecting a book, join its dedicated discussion forum where you can interact with other members, share your thoughts, and practice your English writing.


We also host weekly video chat sessions for verbal practice and deeper book discussions. Don't worry if you're not confident in your English skills yet – our community is supportive and encouraging. Remember, the goal is to improve your English while enjoying great literature. Happy reading and learning!
